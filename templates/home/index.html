Our value

Bubble tea (also known as pearl milk tea, bubble milk tea, or boba) is a tea-based drink invented in Taiwan during the 1980s, which is shaken with ice to create the "bubbles", a foamy layer on top of the drink; chewy tapioca balls ("boba" or "pearls") are added as well. Ice-blended versions are frozen and put into a blender, resulting in a slushy consistency.There are many varieties of the drink with a wide range of flavors. The two most popular varieties are black pearl milk tea and green pearl milk tea.
